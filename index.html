Martina Marino,
passionate about using data
to shape a smarter world.

Research Fellow at the University of Pisa under the project 'wAIne – Viticoltura e Intelligenza Artificiale'. wAIne - supported by Google.org and selected by the Fondo per la Repubblica Digitale - intends to develop an innovative Decision Support System (DSS) for sustainable vineyard management. This project, proposed by the University of Pisa - Polo Universitario Sistemi Logistici di Livorno in collaboration with the Department of Agricultural, Food, and Environmental Sciences and Wondersys, will make use of AI and hyperspectral data in monitoring the phytosanitary status of vines.
